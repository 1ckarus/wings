Get Ready for Zordon

School can be a very tough thing at times, and keeping track of all your assignments, exam dates , as well as your grades can be just as tough. Students should have to focus on school and not some of the stressful baggage it brings. Have no fear Zordon is hear to help! Zordon is going to be the best organizational tool for students. Add courses an updates scores on assignments, exams, projects and whatever else and always know how you are doing in a class.

Features

Along with always knowing how you are doing in all your courses, Zordon has gone the extra mile to keep school simple with some its awesome features.

Dynamic Grade Bar

Its not just enough to know how well you are doing in your classes individually, so we want you to always know what your grade for the semester or quarter is. This is possible with our Dynamic Grade Bar or DGB. The DGB will look at your grades in all your classes and give you in essence your grade point average for that quarter.

Task Management

Have trouble remembering what assignments to do? Do you forget when your exams are and resort to last minute studying the night before? If so, Zordon's task manager will solve your problems. As soon as your find out your exams date, enter it in the task manager and you will be notified in advanced before your exam. Also add assignments and projects and never have to worry about turning something in late again.
